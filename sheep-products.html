Key Indian Sheep Breeds for Meat

India is home to diverse sheep breeds adapted to various climatic regions. Freshflow Foods sources premium sheep meat from these carefully selected breeds, ensuring superior quality for domestic and international markets.

North Temperate Region

J&K, Himachal Pradesh
  • ✓ Gaddi – Dual-purpose (meat and wool)
  • ✓ Changthangi – Hardy, high-altitude breed
  • ✓ Rampur Bushair – Good meat quality and adaptability
  • ✓ Karnah – Suitable for meat and wool

North-Western Arid Region

Rajasthan, Gujarat
  • ✓ Marwari – Hardy desert breed with good meat yield
  • ✓ Magra – Resilient breed, carpet wool type
  • ✓ Malpura – Popular meat breed
  • ✓ Chokla – Heat-tolerant and fast-growing
  • ✓ Jaisalmeri – Drought-resistant breed

Southern Peninsular Region

AP, TN, Karnataka
  • ✓ Nellore – Tallest Indian sheep breed, premium meat
  • ✓ Mandya – High-quality mutton breed
  • ✓ Vembur – Adapted to dry regions
  • ✓ Madras Red – Fast-growing meat breed
  • ✓ Deccani – Widely reared for meat

Eastern Region

Odisha, West Bengal
  • ✓ Ganjam – Suitable for meat and wool
  • ✓ Balangir – Adaptable breed with steady meat yield
  • ✓ Garole – Small-sized breed, meat and wool
